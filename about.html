About John

John was born and raised in Indianapolis, Indiana on his family's farm. Growing up, John cared for cattle, baled hay, and spent most of his time with his close family. As he got older, John began to develop a fascination with the technology on the farm and yearned to understand how things worked from tractors to hydraulic log splitters.

To turn that fascination into a profession, he attended Purdue University and received his Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2014. During the final year of his undergraduate studies, John applied and was accepted into the Harvard Business School 2+2 Program. This deferred acceptance program will allow John to attend HBS starting any year up to 2019.

To continue to build his technical skills before business school, John started working at Eli Lilly and Company in Indianapolis as a Medical Device Engineer after graduating from Purdue. John quickly realized he desired more hands-on responsibility at work and decided to take a risk, leave Lilly, and join DoorDash (at the time a relatviely new start-up with only ~125 employees). A year later, John is still working at DoorDash and is excited about the opportunities to come.

Currently, John lives in downtown Indianapolis with his girlfriend Julia.
